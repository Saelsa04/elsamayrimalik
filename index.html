About ME

My name is Elsa Mayori Malik, I am 25 years old and currently live in Tangerang. I currently work in the field of Network and Telecommunications. I am employed at one of the IT solutions provider vendors, PT. Nusa Network Prakasa.

I am a Diploma graduate in Telecommunications from Akademi Telkom Jakarta, class of 2020. My academic background has provided me with a strong foundation in communication systems, network fundamentals, and applied technologies relevant to the telecommunications industry.

Currently, I serve as a Product Specialist at PT. Nusa Network Prakarsa, a company that focuses on providing IT solutions as a vendor. My main responsibilities include having in-depth knowledge of the products offered to users, providing price quotations, and coordinating with distributors and principals.

Before taking on my current role, I worked for nearly two years as an IT Administrator at PT. Mandiri Utama Finance (MUF). During that time, I gained experience in IT administration, handling all administrative tasks related to the provision of IT equipment, including both hardware and software.

I am committed to continuous professional development and to delivering high-quality support in the workplace. I value attention to detail, efficiency, and collaboration in every project I undertake, and I strive to make meaningful contributions to the success of the organization.
